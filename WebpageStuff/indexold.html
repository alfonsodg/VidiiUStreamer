About

VidiiU Streamer turns your computer into a server ready to stream all your videos over to your WiiU.

On the WiiU videos are presented in a easy to navigate list.

Current Features

  • Play your mp4 videos on the WiiU.
  • Sorts videos of the form Show.Name.S01E01... together into shows with different sections for each season
  • Every episode has its own Thumbnail
  • Videos downloaded in the last 2 days have a 'new' tag

Future Features

  • Support for Transcoding (turning videos that aren't playable on the WiiU into videos that are)
  • Improved GUI, the current one is quite...lacking. (Also options like port config, file names ect)
